A psychological exploration of Taylor Swift’s lyrics — and the selves they unravel.

What this is

This site is an ongoing psychological investigation into Taylor Swift’s lyricism.

Not a fan blog.
Not a shrine.

A study.

Of narrative identity.
Of emotional patterning.
Of the stories we tell ourselves and the ones we inherit.

And of the ways we self-destruct — and rebuild — across our own eras.
